Experiment Description	Whole exome sequencing data was produced and analyzed by Baylor Human Genome Sequencing Center as part of the TCGA effort towards comprehensive genomic characterization of multiple tumor types.  Massively parallel sequencing exome capture was performed on untreated clear cell renal cell carcinoma samples using NimbleGen (custom designed) arrays according the manufacturer’s instructions. All exome sequencing was performed on the ABS SOLiD and Illumina HiSeq platforms. Basic alignment and initial sequence analysis were carried out using the Mercury analysis pipeline.
